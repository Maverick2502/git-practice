Here's a short synopsis for Star Wars:

In a galaxy far, far away, the tyrannical Galactic Empire tightens its grip, but a spark of hope arises through the Rebel Alliance. Luke Skywalker, a young farm boy with a mysterious destiny, joins forces with Princess Leia, rogue smuggler Han Solo, and Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi to fight against the evil Sith Lord Darth Vader and destroy the Empire's ultimate weapon, the Death Star. Along the way, Luke begins to unlock the power of the Force, setting the stage for an epic battle between good and evil that will shape the galaxy's fate.
